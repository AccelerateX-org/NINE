# Introduction

:::note
Education is a question of attitude, digitalization is human-centered.
:::


hamSTER is supporting students and lecturers organising their daily life at the university:

- supporting students compiling their personal schedule, recognizing the process of study and personal interests
- supporting lecturers planing, preparing and publishing all information about courses and examinations in a single place

Administrative tasks like planing, seat booking are widely distributed to the corresponding roles. Self-organisation by distributed administration and cooperation. Centralized administration is possible, but not required for running the lectures and exams.

hamSTER is open source, published under MIT licence. Feel free hosting hamSTER on your own infrastructure. For professional services refer to https://accelartex.org.

Interested in a demo? just look at https://nine.hm.edu
