# Configure locations

Where activities take place.

:::info
hamSTER does not replace facillity management or booking system. Here there are the system boundaries.
Only information really necessary for organising the activities are managed here.
:::


## Campus and Buildings

Not implemented yet - no need

### Rooms

A room is identified its number, i.g. the label outside of the room used for navigation.

Rooms are assigned to organisers for representing availability.

Rooms are assigned to dates of any kind of activity.

## Virtual Rooms

Personal equipement of members representing "virtual room", i.e. Zoom, Teams. Just an URL and a name.

Virtual rooms can be assigned by its owner to dates of activties.
