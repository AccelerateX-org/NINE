# Define the formal standards

Formal standards are describing the portfolio of *business activities* of an instiution.
In general these elements are described in legal documents ("Ordnung", "Satzung", "Richtlinien") of the institution

- "Allgemeine (Studien- und) Prüfungsordnung"
- "Akkreditierungsgrundlagen", "QM-Plan"

## Degrees

Examples:

- Bachelor of Engineering
- Bachelor of Arts
- Master of Science
